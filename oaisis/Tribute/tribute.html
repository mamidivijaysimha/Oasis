Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action." - A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Biography

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on 15th October 1931 and passed away on 27th July 2015. He was the 11th President of India and a renowned aerospace scientist. Dr. Kalam made significant contributions to India's space and missile programs and became widely known as the "Missile Man of India." His life and work continue to inspire millions across the globe.

Achievements

  • Played a key role in the development of India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV).
  • Led the development of ballistic missile technology in India.
  • Served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Recipient of India's highest civilian awards including Bharat Ratna, Padma Vibhushan, and Padma Bhushan.
